About Us

The Mechatronics and Robotics Society (MARS) at UVA is a student-run, experimental engineering club dedicated to creating leaders in STEM.

Every year, we build a janky moon rover for the NASA Robotic Mining Competition. This year, our robot will mine regolith to build berms.

  • Gain hands-on experience in design, machining, circuitry, and coding
  • Practice organizing and collaborating with a professinal engineering team
  • Volunteer to promote STEM in the Charlottesville community
  • Based out of Lacy Hall in Charlottesville, Virginia
